Who we are

Mruchem is an emerging manufacturer and exporter of Speciality Chemicals headquartered in Vadodara, Gujarat-India.

Mruchem was founded by Mr. Mrugesh Parekh, an entrepreneur with vivid experience of working with emerging companies in Chemical Sector and has extensive reach to global markets.

With gamut of ingredient offerings in Cosmetics, Flavor, Fragrance and Pharma Industry; Mruchem emerges as one stop solution for globally spreaded end users.

Mruchem is making a difference for it’s customers by consistently serving Quality chemicals at Competitive prices and within Committed delivery schedules.
